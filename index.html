About Us

Who We Are

MVFPro International Manpower Services is a Philippine-land based employment agency, duly licensed by the Philippine Overseas Employment Administration (POEA). With License No. POEA 004-LB-013017-PL and registered with the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI). We recognize the Filipino dream. We understand the Filipino struggle. We know that the journey to a brighter future is indeed long and arduous, but nothing that the perseverance of the Filipino people cannot overcome. We at MVFPRO, as master recruiters and seasoned industry experts, are here to provide you with a solid foundation to build your dream. Our specialists have been staffing multinational companies all over the globe for the past two decades, establishing lasting personal and business relationships through efficient and equitable servicing.

The Leadership

Although the company only started in 2017, its founder and President, Ms. Myra V. Fuentes, already has two decades of recruitment experience under her belt. Ms. Fuentes has a solid trackrecord in the manpower and overseas employment industry, having been the Vice Chairman and Marketing Director of 7107 Islands Placement & Promotion, Inc.; Operations Manager/Owner of Worldwiser international Manpower Agency; and, Owner/Incorporator of Jerzon International Manpower & Recruitment Agency.

Our Mission

To provide world-class recruitment services to clients, and give Filipinos quality jobs abroad.

Our Vision

To be the link that connects the Filipino to their dreams, giving them an equal opportunity in the international workplace.
